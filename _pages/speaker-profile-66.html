Speaker Profile

Brian Luft

Brian has been developing software professionally for more than 10 years and has a wide range of experience. He has been an active contributor in the Python/Django community, including speaking at DjangoCon and presenting at LA area meetups. He was one of the first Lincoln Loop developers and has been with the agency for more than 2 years.
