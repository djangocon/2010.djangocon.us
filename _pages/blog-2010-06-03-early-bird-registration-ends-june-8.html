Early Bird Registration Ends June 8

3rd June 2010 by Steve Holden

Make sure you take advantage of the special pricing.

Just a quick reminder that DjangoCon 2010 early bird rates end on June 8. If you want to save on your registration please make sure you register straight away.

Early bird rates are offered to encourage early registration so we can get some idea of the likely attendance at the conference. It gives you a great reduction on the price, so register now while you still have a chance to save money!
