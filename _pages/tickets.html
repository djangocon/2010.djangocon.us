Registration is open! Register on EventBrite.

IMPORTANT NOTE: After August 13 there is no guarantee that the conference hotel rate will continue to be available. To reserve accommodation at the most advantageous rate please email our conference organizer with your requirements.

Early-bird registration ended on June 8th, 2010. Thanks to everyone who registered early!

What does it cost?

Regular
Corporate $595
Individual $495
Student $210

Which rate is right for me?

If you're paying your own way to DjangoCon US, pay the individual rate.

If your company is paying for you to attend, please register at the corporate rate. This helps keep the conference affordable to all, especially students and those needing financial aid.

Government employees should register at the corporate rate.

The student rate is for full-time students only; you'll need to bring proof of your status as a student (i.e. a student ID card) to pick up your ticket.

What's included?

Registration includes admission to the conference, hotel internet access, breakfast, lunch, and morning and afternoon break refreshments on all three conference days. You are also invited to three social events, one each evening of the conference (we regret that minors cannot be admitted to the Tuesday night drinks social at the Standard bar).

Hotel registration is separate; see venue for details. Be sure you use the link on the venues page or in your registration confirmation to get the conference hotel rates. NOTE: the conference room block is now sold out. Please email Nancy Asche to reserve accommodation at the conference hotel at the best available price.
