Venue

DjangoCon US will again be held at the Doubletree Hotel in Portland, Oregon.

Location

Doubletree Hotel Portland
Lloyd Center 1000 NE Multnomah Street
Portland, OR 97232

Phone: 503-281-6111

Rates

Both single and double occupancy rooms will be: $104.

A block of rooms have been reserved for September 5th–12th. The special room rate will be available until August 6th or until the group block is sold-out, whichever comes first.

Please book your hotel room on the DjangoCon Group Hotel Booking Page. This automatically gives you the conference rate as long as it remains available, and allows us to verify you as a part of the conference room block.

Directions

From the airport via MAX Light Rail: Board MAX Red Line to City Center & Beaverton TC. Get off at Lloyd Center/NE 11th Ave. MAX Station. Walk half a block to the hotel. Cost $2.05

From the airport: Merge on I-205 South. Take the I-84 West exit toward Portland City Center. Take Exit 1, Lloyd Center (Lloyd Blvd). Turn right at the third signal (9th Ave). The hotel is one block up on the left.
